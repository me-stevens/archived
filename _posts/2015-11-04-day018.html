Today I learnt

That I should not spent more that 15-20 minutes sketching the design of my application. Because the tests are going to change everything anyway!

When I first deigned my Tic Tac Toe, I spent a lot of time thinking about the classes I would need, the responsibilities of each, the collaborators, even the methods and fields I would need! Not only all that changed from the moment I started sketching until I (thought I) had the final design, but also when I started to code, many things changed.

However, I do think sketching on a piece of paper helps me write code with less bugs. At least in those days when I knew nothing about TDD. And it also helps me visualise things more clearly and organize the classes.
