Other things

I slipped an innocent "maybe it's because I'm stupid" and Rabea bitch-slapped me again. I told her that I am honest, and that is honestly how I feel every day. I don't even think about it, I have it so assimilated that the sentence comes out of my lips without thinking.

Then Georgina also told me to stop saying that, because, if a beginner hears me say that, they will be like "Oh, if the squid is stupid then, what am I?". Jarkyn said that I can't be stupid because I have a PhD and I am a Physicist, to which the others agreed.

I think they have a point, and I can see their point. But I never say that I have a PhD or that I am a Physicist, because then people suddenly think you are a genius or something, and that is not true. That's because people normally have no idea about what it is that researchers do, or think scientists are these mystic beings that have a huge brain. Nothing further away from reality!

I am not in the mood to write a rant about that topic now... maybe in some future post (that will never come). It brings bad memories. But what's a physicist even? If anything, studying Physics was A LOT OF WORK. Nothing else. No brains needed! And what's a researcher even? Again, you don't need brains to be a researcher. You just need to be a masochist. The most accurate description of a researchers's life is probably in the PhD Comics series. If you read them all, you will know.

Anyway, I'm going to listen to Rabea and try to stop saying bad things about myself.
