Today I learnt

This Monday's Zagaku talk was by Enrique, and he showed us a new method to design your app: the CRC cards. CRC stands for "Class Responsibility Collaborator". The model is about using paper cards divided into three sections: name of the class at the top, responsabilities of the class at the left, and classes that interact with that class to the right. For me the most interesting thing about this technique is, that it allows you to detect class couplings (when you see the collaborator of one class also has the class as its collaborator), apart of helping you organize your code. Also, I'm the kind of person who writes things down, so anything that has to do with using a pen and a paper is cool with me.
