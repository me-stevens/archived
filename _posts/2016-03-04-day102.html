Today was Friday, and on Friday's I can't get anything done. But today there was another reason to not get anything done.

TODAY WAS THE DAY

THE DAY WHEN JARKYN BECAME A CRAFTSWOMAN

AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHHHHH!!!!

And so no apprentice did anything today. We were just too nervous to concentrate in anything. We had lunch and then watched Jarkyn's talk, which was awesome, very calm, very thought provoking and with a lot of deep messages. It also had some funny moments and it made me feel relieved to see Jarkyn laugh.

I was as nervous as for Mateu's big day some months ago. I was up there with her! After the talk I gave her a huge hug and we went with Rabea, Priya and Sarah for a walk to take some fresh air, have some tea and pastries, and have some good laughs.

When we were back, CJ told us that in Chicago, the whole thing was actually worse and you had to wait for much longer. That made me remember what LaToya Allen said when she was interviewed for the CodeNewbie podcast (min 19:30). She thought the challenges were too hardcore and she left the company after one year of apprenticeship to join a product oriented company.

I've been joking with Jarkyn that the key of her craftsmanship was in my belly, and she had to do a Saw move and cut my belly to get the key. I did it in order for her to compare that with what she had to do for her challenges and think "This is nothing, compared to killing the squid!". So Georgina asked me if my belly was healing, and I told Jarkyn "Oh, no, I'm alive, you are not gonna pass your challenges!" and we had a good laugh.

We were in this state of excitement and anticipation, we all knew she was in, but anyway, until we heard it from Uku's lips, it wasn't real. And then he said it. And it was for realz. The announcement suddenly took some weight out of my shoulders and some pressure out of my chest. It was so relieving! And I fought to contain my manly tears, but I couldn't, so I thought, Fuck it. Welcome happiness manly tears. We've been in a roller coaster the whole fucking day. The whole week!

Anyway, I had been waiting for this day for so long, and now Jarkyn is finally a craftsperson, or craftswoman (she said she didn't like the sound of craftsperson).

I'M SO HAPPYYYYYYYYYYYYYYYYYYYY

You have worked your ass off, you have spent so many sleepless nights, you have struggled, if anybody deserves to be there is you. Embrace it Jarkyn, go for it, its yours, forever.
